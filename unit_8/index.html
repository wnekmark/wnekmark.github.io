Alternative Brand Perspectives

Saturday August 6th 2016, New York

One of the funniest things I've witnessed over the past couple years in and around the ad agency business is the hilarious reductio ad absurdum that's been going on around the actual word 'advertising' itself.

It reminds me of something a supermodel friend of mine once said as she witheringly laid waste the would-be dietary 'miracle' of fruit and veggie smoothies .

"Trust me, poppet" she said, "if it isn't water it's food."

Fairfax Cone, founder of the original Foote, Cone, Belding (FCB), said something that, once heard, blows away all the current self-serving, tortuous, cod-revolutionary, faddism in marketing and marketing services every bit as effectively:

"Advertising is what you do when you can't go see someone. That's all it is."
