About

The movie Suicide Squad passes $500 million after only 13 days, a testament to a powerful idea as old as ideas: when there's BIG trouble and none of the usual measures work, a radically different kind of thinking and action is needed; people outside the normal bounds of endeavor; wild cards, insurgents, left-of-field thinkers.

Welcome to Alternative Brand Perspectives, a group brought together by Cannes Advertising Grand Prix-winning creative director Mark Wnek around one single guiding principal: when the chips are down, with only a couple grains of sand remaining in the top of the hourglass, nothing less than a laser-focused blast of the very best creative and strategic thinking will do.
